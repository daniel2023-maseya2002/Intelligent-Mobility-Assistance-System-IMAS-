About IMAS

Revolutionizing Urban Mobility

The Intelligent Mobility Assistance System (IMAS) is designed to transform public transportation in Kinshasa. Our innovative platform provides real-time information, smart trip planning, and enhanced accessibility features to make your journey smoother and more efficient.

We believe that everyone deserves access to reliable, convenient, and sustainable transportation solutions. IMAS bridges the gap between traditional public transport and modern technology, creating a seamless experience for all passengers.

Our system not only benefits individual travelers but also helps transport operators optimize their services, reduce costs, and improve overall system efficiency.
